Hi! My name is Núbia


I am a native Carioca (resident of Rio) and I have been teaching Brazilian Portuguese to foreigners for nearly 20 years. I studed Portuguese at Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro and did my Post-graduate in Literature at the same University. I've given lessons to students from all over the world, and all age groups. After years of work, I've developed my own method of teaching Portuguese. Language is culture! And I specialize in the understanding of the social and historical contexts of Brazil and their social relations as a path to manage communication with Brazilians. Currently, I'm offering group or individual classes - they can be online, or, if you are in Rio de Janeiro, it can be at your residence, your workplace, or various other places in the city. If you're interested, please email fill the form with your course choices and pesonal information.
